RTA (ready-to-assemble) cabinets ship flat and require you to build each cabinet before installation — that's 45 minutes or more per cabinet. Express Kitchens assembled cabinets arrive fully built, doors on and drawers in, ready to go straight to your walls. No tools, no instructions, no lost weekend.
All our cabinet boxes are constructed from ¾" solid plywood — not particle board or MDF. Plywood is stronger, holds screws better, and resists moisture.
